User Experience at athenahealth

I worked as a user experience designer at athenahealth from 2014 to 2017.

While at athena, the user experience team at athena worked collaboratively with project managers and developers to create value for its users. Specifically, I spent my entire athena career on athenaClinicals, our electronic health record (EHR)

The majority of my time I have been an embedded UX member of the Chart team, whose purpose is to enable users easy and seamless access to patient information while allowing users to understand the patient's story.

My portfolio contains a long-term research project I helped lead, and I have a few additional examples of my work here: Medication list sorting Multispecialty support MultiSelect within the Chart

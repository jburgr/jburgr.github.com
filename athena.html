Where am I now?

I've been working as a user experience designer at athenahealth since graduating from Olin College in 2014.

UX at athena works collaboratively with project managers and developers to create value for its users. Specifically, I've spent my entire athena career on athenaClinicals, our electronic health record (EHR)

The majority of my time I have been an embedded UX member of the Chart team, whose purpose is to enable users easy and seamless access to patient information while allowing users to understand the patient's story.

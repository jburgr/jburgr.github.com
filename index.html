Hi there

I'm an engineer-turned-Product Designer. Currently, I am a design researcher at PathAI, working on their suite of clinical products. Previously I have worked as a product designer at DrugDev on their LMS to ensure clinical trials' staff are fully trained and compliant. Prior to that, I lived in the Netherlands as a Fulbright Scholar, where I took courses at TU Delft to increase my knowledge of the Dutch Design methodologies. Before that, I worked as a senior user experience design associate at athenahealth. I received my B.S. in Engineering with a Concentration in Biomechanics from Olin College of Engineering in 2014.
